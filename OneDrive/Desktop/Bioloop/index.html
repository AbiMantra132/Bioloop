In the heart of Tabanan, Bali’s rice granary, Bioloop was born from a simple act of care — giving new life to what was once considered waste.

We work hand in hand with local farmers to transform rice husks into the core material for our biodegradable cards, while empowering waste collectors and nearby communities to gather dried leaves that we refine into natural fibers.

Every Bioloop card is more than an eco-friendly product, it’s a symbol of collaboration between farmers, communities, and Indonesia’s green innovators, turning waste into
hope, and sustainability into reality.
